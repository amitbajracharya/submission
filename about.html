Our Origin

Estate & Co. began its journey in 1984 as a boutique litigation firm situated in the heart of New York’s financial district. In an era of rapid deregulation and shifting economic structures, our founders recognized a need for a legal practice that operated with the same structural integrity as the skyscrapers rising around them.

What started as a focused litigation house has evolved into a global authority. We have systematically expanded our footprint and our expertise, yet our methodology remains unchanged: analyze the foundation, pressure-test the framework, and execute with surgical precision. Today, we stand as a beacon of architectural juris, managing complex legal infrastructures across continents.
